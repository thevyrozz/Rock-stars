Rock Stars

Jerome John Garcia

August 1, 1942 - August 9, 1995

American musician known for being the principal songwriter, lead guitarist, and a vocalist with the rock band Grateful Dead.

Tina Turner

November 26, 1939 - May 24, 2023

Known as the "Queen of Rock 'n' Roll", Tina rose to fame as the lead singer of Ike & Tina Turner and later became a solo icon.

Frank Vincent Zappa

December 21, 1940 - December 4, 1993

Known for his nonconformity, free-form improvisation, and satire of American culture, Zappa was a true musical icon.

Dolores O'Riordan

September 6, 1971 - January 15, 2018

Lead vocalist and lyricist of the alternative rock band The Cranberries.

Gwen Stefani

October 3, 1969

Co-founder and lead vocalist of the band No Doubt, known for hits like "Just a Girl" and "Spiderwebs".

Elvis Presley

January 8, 1935 - August 16, 1977

Known as the "King of Rock and Roll," Elvis is one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century.
